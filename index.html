Frequently Asked Questions

The course is designed to be completed over a span of 7 months, with a commitment of approximately 5-7 hours per week. Each week includes video lectures, reading materials, and assignments that help reinforce the concepts learned, while the course is self-paced, we encourage participate to stay on track with the weekly schedule to maximize their learning experience.

By the end of the course, you will have the skills to build several web applications. Examples include a **Personal portfolio website** that showcase your projects and skills, a **dynamic e-commerce platform** with user authentication and payment integration, and **real-time chat application** using webSockets. Additionally, you will learn to create a **task management tool** similar to Trello, using ReactJS for the front-end. NodeJS and ExpressJS for the backend, and MongoDB for the database. You may also build small applications that integrate with third-party APIs. Such as **weather dashboard** that fetches real-time data from a weather API. The final project will be a comprehensive application that incorporates all these skills, serving as a capstone to demonstrate your proficiency and creativity. Providing you with a portfolio piece to show potential employers or client.

This course is designed for beginners, so no prior programming experience is required. However, a basic of understanding of how to use a computer and navigate the internet is helpful. We also recommend having a grasp of problem-solving skills and logical thinking. All necessary software tools and resources will be provided, and you will be guided step-by-step through the setup and use of the each one.

Throughout the course, you will have access to a range of support options. These include a dedicated course forum where you can ask questions and get help from instructors and fellow students weekly live Q&A sessions with the course instructor, and 24/7 access to all course materials. If you encounter any technical issues or have questions about the course content, our support team is available to assist you via email or chat.
